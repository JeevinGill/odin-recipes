Sandwich

Description

This sandwich recipe uses every day ingredients and fresh bread.

The ingredients and steps below can be adjusted to fit your preferences.

For example, you can swap our whole wheat bread with white bread.

To ensure a crisp sandwich, we recommend you use a toaster.

Ingredients

  • Whole wheat bread -- 2 slices
  • Lettuce
  • Red onions
  • Green peppers
  • Avacado
  • Tomatoes
  • Mayo
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Toast the bread. Once toasted remove and place on wire rack to avoid sogginess
  2. Cut red onions, green peppers, and tomatoes
  3. Cut avacado and use a fork to make into paste
  4. Spread mayo and mustard onto bread
  5. Add ingredients as desired onto bread
  6. Enjoy!
