Scrambled Eggs

Description

This is a basic recipe for delicious scrambled eggs.

You may choose to add to remove specific ingredients depending on your tastes.

The most important thing is using quality eggs!

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • Onions
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2% milk
  • Toast (optional)

Steps

  1. Cut onions
  2. Crack eggs into a large bowl
  3. Add salt and pepper to taste
  4. Add chopped onions
  5. Use a whisk to beat eggs and other ingredients together
  6. Add 100ml of 2% milk
  7. Use whisk to mix in milk
  8. Pour eggs into pan heated to medium
  9. Let eggs begin to settle around eggs
  10. Continously pull eggs that are setting from edges into middle
  11. Continuing this scrambling technique until eggs are solid
  12. Remove from pan and enjoy!
